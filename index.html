Profit Share Calculator

Calculate Corporate Factor first, then use it with PF and Base Salary to estimate profit share instantly.

How To Use This

Step 1: Enter your PF as a decimal value such as 0.8, 0.9 or 1.0.

Step 2: Enter your Base Salary as a normal dollar amount, for example 85000.

Step 3: Either calculate Corporate Factor using ROIC, Organic Growth and Acquired Growth, or enter Corporate Factor directly as a percentage without the % symbol.

Step 4: The calculator converts the Corporate Factor percentage into a decimal for the final bonus calculation.

Example: If Corporate Factor is 16.2%, PF is 1.0 and Base Salary is $85,000, the bonus is $13,770.00.
