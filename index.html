How To Use This

Step 1: If needed, calculate a Corporate Factor on the Corporate Factor Builder page.

Step 2: Enter the Corporate Factor here as a percentage, for example 16.2 for 16.2%.

Step 3: Enter your PF as a decimal value such as 0.8, 0.9 or 1.0.

Step 4: Enter your Base Salary as a normal dollar amount, for example 85000.

Example: If Corporate Factor is 16.2%, PF is 1.0 and Base Salary is $85,000, the bonus is $13,770.00.
